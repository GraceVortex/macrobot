================================================================================
                         THE PROBLEM WITH PHASE 3
================================================================================

Phase 3 reported impressive results but WITHOUT Sharpe calculation.
Other phases used PER-TRADE Sharpe which INFLATES the metric.

OLD METHOD (Per-Trade Sharpe):
Formula: mean(trade_returns) / std(trade_returns) * sqrt(252/2)
Assumption: ~126 independent trading periods per year
Problem: Ignores actual calendar time between trades

This method can show Sharpe 3-7 but is NOT comparable to industry benchmarks!


================================================================================
                       THE INSTITUTIONAL FIX
================================================================================

NEW METHOD (Daily Sharpe):
1. Build daily equity curve (all calendar days)
2. Calculate daily P&L (including zero-return days)
3. Formula: mean(daily_returns) / std(daily_returns) * sqrt(252)

This is the STANDARD industry calculation used by:
• Hedge funds
• Mutual funds  
• Academic research
• Risk committees

It accounts for actual TIME, not just number of trades.
