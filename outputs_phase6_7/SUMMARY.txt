================================================================================
                         ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
================================================================================

WHY DID STRATEGY FAIL?

1. REGIME DEPENDENCY:
   Works: High volatility (VIX >20), clear trends
   Fails: Normal volatility (VIX <15), mixed signals
   
   Problem: Can't predict when high vol will return

2. SMALL SAMPLE SIZE:
   53 trades over 10 years = ~5 per year
   12 trades in 2015-2019 = insufficient edge
   
   Problem: Strategy too infrequent to be reliable

3. WEAK ALPHA IN NORMAL MARKETS:
   2015-2019 Sharpe: 0.07 (near zero)
   Only works in crisis/transition periods
   
   Problem: Not a robust all-weather strategy

4. HIGH TRANSACTION COSTS:
   0.19% per trade on avg +1.74% = 11% of gross profit
   Small edge gets eaten by costs
   
   Problem: Alpha too small for cost structure
